Race to Santa

I missed December's Ludum Dare, but I still wanted to make a game in that spirit. So, I picked my own theme of "2 player - same keyboard" and gave myself 3 days of my holiday break to make a game in that theme. I wanted something I could show up with on Christmas and play with my family.

"Race to Santa" is what I came up with. It's a 2 player, same keyboard, ice skating, racing game.

While I was figuring out the skating mechanic I put together a demo page that you can see here. Once the skating was tuned, I moved on to making a game out of it.
